SECURIHEAT D-LIST
Line type heat detector
LIST
Безграничная эффективность противопожарной защиты

The SecuriSens d-LIST line type heat detector is a resettable line type heat detector designed to European product standard EN 54-5. Line type heat detectors consist of a sensor control unit (SCU 800), sensor element (SEC 15 sensor cable) and the functional element (sensors). In the case of the SecuriSens d-LIST, the sensor element consists of an electrical multipoint sensor cable with a multitude of temperature sensors. Line type heat detectors are used for fire detection purposes primarily in areas where conventional fire detectors cannot be used due to aggressive and critical ambient conditions.

Посмотреть видео о системах

Система применяется во всем мире:

  • Cable conduits and supply tunnels, e.g. at power plants or airports
  • Multi-storey car parks, underground car parks
  • Cold stores and freezers
  • Industrial plant surveillance e.g. in conveyor plants, production lines
  • Loading ramps
  • Refineries, gas lines and district heating lines
  • Incineration plants, waste disposal installations
  • Saw mills, farms
  • Road, railway and underground railway tunnels

Фотографии SecuriSens d-LIST

Режим работы системы

The SecuriSens d-LIST system is based on acquiring data from numerous temperature sensors which are integrated in the sensor cable at regular intervals. The system measures both the actual temperature of the surroundings (convective heat) as well as the infrared radiation (radiant heat). Thanks to these characteristics and the measuring cycle of 10 seconds, the response time of the line type heat detector is extremely short. The intelligent SCU 800 sensor control unit evaluates the measurement data and determines whether an alarm or pre-signal is present based on the specific programming. The LISTp800 programming software is a flexible programming environment which makes it possible to program the sensor control unit easily according to the project-specific requirements. The optionally available programmable relays can be used for actuating the necessary systems in the event of an alarm or fault.

Преимущества использования системы:

Lightning-fast detection through IR sensitivity
Maximum group programming flexibility with regard to different sensitivity ranges
Clear and precise localisation through fixed sensor positions and IR sensitivity
Installation across fire sections
Networking possibilities for larger premises
Output of the temperature profile
Maintenance free
